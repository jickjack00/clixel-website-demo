About Us


Who We Are
Clixel is a dynamic game development studio based in Eastbourne, dedicated to crafting immersive and innovative gaming experiences. With a passion for storytelling, cutting-edge technology, and player engagement, Clixel specialises in developing unique games that captivate audiences across a variety of platforms.



How We Co-Operate
Our team of talented developers, designers, and artists brings creativity and technical expertise together to deliver projects that stand out in the competitive gaming industry. At Clixel, we thrive on pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment, blending compelling narratives with visually stunning worlds and gameplay mechanics that resonate with players worldwide.



What We Do
We make innovative games that redefine the genre they were created in, providing a refreshing experience even for a genre that seems like it has reached its limit, but for Clixel Games, there's no such thing as a limit. Each game of ours has a gripping story, detailed and attractive graphics, smooth and satisfying gameplay, head-bumping sound design, unique and interesting ideas, and several more to make the games as fun as they can be.



Clixel values collaboration and community, actively fostering partnerships with local talent, colleges, and industry experts to inspire the next generation of game creators.
